# Dev Master Runbook

## Mission

Lead day-to-day execution for the sprint backlog and keep the team aligned on delivery.

## Responsibilities

- Drive backlog refinement with the Scrum Master and Product Owner.
- Host daily stand-ups and maintain the `Ready → In Progress → QA Gate` flow.
- Ensure stories meet Definition of Ready before starting.
- Pair with QA Master during dry-run stories and complex reviews.
- Communicate blockers and mitigation plans within 1 business day.

## Daily Flow

1. Review prioritized issues in the sprint project view.
2. Confirm WIP limits are respected; move completed tasks to `QA Gate`.
3. Sync with Automation Wrangler on CI/CD health.
4. Post stand-up summary in the sprint issue.

## Escalation

- **Dependency blockage >24h:** Notify Scrum Master and Product Owner.
- **Guardrail failure:** Coordinate fix with QA Master and Compliance Sentinel.
- **Scope creep:** Raise change request before accepting new work.
